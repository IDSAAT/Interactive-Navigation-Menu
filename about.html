What We Built

This navigation menu stays fixed at the top of every page and changes appearance when you scroll or hover.

The menu background becomes darker and adds a shadow when you scroll down, then returns to transparent when you scroll back to top.

Technologies Used

  • HTML5 for structure
  • CSS3 for styling and transitions
  • JavaScript for scroll detection and dynamic class toggling
